## üìù Syncing Tips

### **Perfect Sync:**

1. **Start Together:**
   - Align audio start with video start
   - Both should begin at 0:00

2. **Check Key Moments:**
   - 0:15 - "Battle Arena" should match modal opening
   - 0:45 - "Kiddies Mode" should match click
   - 1:00 - "Step one" should match Step 1 appearing
   - 2:30 - "Thank you" should match closing

3. **Fine-Tune:**
   - If audio is ahead: Move audio right (later)
   - If audio is behind: Move audio left (earlier)
   - Adjust by 0.1-0.5 seconds at a time

4. **Test Multiple Times:**
   - Play through entire video
   - Check sync at beginning, middle, end
   - Adjust if needed

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### **Audio Out of Sync:**
- **Solution:** Re-import and align more carefully
- Use frame-by-frame adjustment (arrow keys)
- Check if video/audio have different frame rates

### **Video Too Large:**
- **Solution:** Re-export with lower bitrate
- Use 720p instead of 1080p
- Compress with HandBrake (free)

### **Audio Too Quiet:**
- **Solution:** Increase audio volume in editor
- Most editors have volume slider (0-200%)
- Boost to 120-150% if needed

### **Video Quality Poor:**
- **Solution:** Re-export with higher bitrate
- Check original recording quality
- Use H.264 codec, not older formats

### **Watermark on Video:**
- **Solution:** Use free tier of different tool
- Or pay for one month ($10-20) to remove
- Kapwing Pro: $16/month
- VEED Pro: $18/month
