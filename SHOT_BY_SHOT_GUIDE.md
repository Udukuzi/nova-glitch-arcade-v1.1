## üéØ Recording Tips

### Cursor Movement:
- ‚úÖ **Smooth and deliberate** (not jerky)
- ‚úÖ **Pause on important elements** (2-3 seconds)
- ‚úÖ **Don't rush** (let animations complete)
- ‚úÖ **Highlight key features** (hover to show)

### Pacing:
- ‚úÖ **Let animations finish** before moving on
- ‚úÖ **Pause after each major action** (2 seconds)
- ‚úÖ **Give viewers time to read** text on screen
- ‚úÖ **Don't click too fast**

### What to Avoid:
- ‚ùå Erratic mouse movements
- ‚ùå Clicking before animations complete
- ‚ùå Moving too fast
- ‚ùå Covering important text with cursor
- ‚ùå Going off-screen
