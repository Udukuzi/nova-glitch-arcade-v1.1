### **Problem: AI voice sounds robotic**
**Solution:** 
- Use ElevenLabs (best quality)
- Choose "Adam" or "Antoni" voice
- Adjust stability to 50-60%

### **Problem: Audio out of sync**
**Solution:**
- Re-align in editor
- Use frame-by-frame adjustment

**Solution:**
- Re-export at 720p instead of 1080p
